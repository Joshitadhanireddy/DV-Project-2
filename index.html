Data Visualization Project

Welcome to our Data Visualization Project by Team 8! In this project, we are visualizing data from the Crufts website to uncover interesting trends and insights about the dog show world. Our visualizations include:

  • Visualization 1: Best in Show winners through the decades.
  • Visualization 2: Dog show entries for 2024.
  • Visualization 3: Crufts demographics.

Explore the visualizations to see how breed success, entry trends, and audience demographics have evolved over time!
